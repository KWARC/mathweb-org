MathWeb.org: supporting mathematics on the web!

MathWeb.org supports web-supported mathematics, in particular

  • software systems that connect a wide-range of mathematical services by a common, mathematical software bus.
  • a set of mathematical services that support all aspects of doing mathematics on the web (see the spiral)
  • an inter lingua for mathematical communication (OMDoc)
  • a portal for potential users and a discussion forum for developers

Michael Kohlhase holds the MathWeb.org domain for the purpose specified above. Should it ever be necessary, we will move to some more formalized form of organization.
